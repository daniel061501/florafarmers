About Us

Flora Farmers Cluster is a community-driven agriculture cooperative based in Flora, Apayao. We unite local farmers to share resources, knowledge, and market opportunities while promoting sustainable farming practices that protect our environment and support our community's growth.

Our Projects

Organic Farming Initiative

Promoting chemical-free farming methods to produce healthy, sustainable crops.

Farmer Training Programs

Educational workshops and hands-on training for modern agricultural techniques.

Market Access Support

Connecting farmers directly with consumers and fair trade markets.

About FLOFRACO

Our History

Founded in 2015, Flora Farmers Cluster Agriculture Cooperative (FLOFRACO) began as a small group of dedicated farmers committed to sustainable agriculture. Over the years, we've grown into a thriving cooperative of over 200 members, serving communities across the region with fresh, organic produce and innovative farming solutions.

Our Mission

To empower local farmers through collective action, sustainable farming practices, and market access while ensuring food security and environmental stewardship for current and future generations.

Our Vision

To be the leading agricultural cooperative in sustainable farming, recognized for excellence in organic production, farmer empowerment, and community development.

Our Values

  • Sustainability: Protecting our environment for future generations
  • Community: Working together for mutual benefit
  • Integrity: Honest and transparent in all dealings
  • Innovation: Embracing new technologies and methods
  • Quality: Delivering the highest standard of products and services

Our Projects

Organic Vegetable Farm Expansion

Expanding our organic vegetable production by 50 acres, introducing new varieties of tomatoes, peppers, and leafy greens using advanced greenhouse technology and sustainable irrigation systems.

Ongoing 2024-2025

Smart Farming Initiative

Implementing IoT sensors, automated irrigation, and drone monitoring across 100 acres to optimize water usage, reduce pesticide application, and increase crop yields through precision agriculture.

Planning Phase 2024-2026

Farmer Training Center

Establishing a comprehensive training facility to provide hands-on education in organic farming techniques, sustainable practices, and business management for 500+ farmers annually.

Phase 1 Complete 2023-2025

Waste-to-Wealth Program

Converting agricultural waste into valuable products including organic fertilizer, biogas, and packaging materials, creating additional revenue streams while reducing environmental impact.

Ongoing 2024-2025
